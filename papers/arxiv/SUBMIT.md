# arXiv submission — 3 steps (needs your arXiv account)
1. zip this folder's contents (tex + 4 pngs, no subfolder): the .tex compiles server-side.
2. arxiv.org -> Submit -> new submission -> category cs.LG (cross-list cs.PF) -> upload zip.
   License: arXiv non-exclusive. Author: Federico Sciuca.
3. Abstract: paste from the tex \begin{abstract} block. Comments field suggestion:
   "Code, raw logs, interactive calculator and pre-registration documents at
   github.com/FedericoTs/quantprobe".
Note: first-time cs.LG submitters may need endorsement - the submission page will say.
